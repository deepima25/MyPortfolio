About Me

Hello! I’m Deepima Shrivastav, a passionate Web Developer & Designer with a love for creating visually stunning and highly functional websites. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design, and I enjoy transforming ideas into interactive digital experiences.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked on personal projects, freelance work, and collaborative web apps, focusing on clean code, user experience, and performance optimization. I constantly explore new technologies and design trends to stay up-to-date and deliver innovative solutions.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy photography, UI/UX design inspiration, and learning about new web frameworks. I believe in lifelong learning and enjoy challenging myself to improve both technically and creatively.

Key Highlights:

  • Proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern frameworks
  • Experience in building responsive and mobile-friendly websites
  • Strong understanding of UI/UX principles and accessibility standards
  • Passionate about creative problem-solving and continuous learning

I’m always excited to take on new projects, collaborate with other creative minds, and contribute to the web development community. Feel free to contact me if you’d like to work together or just want to say hello!
